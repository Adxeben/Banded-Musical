The band

WildCats is a popular music band based in Lagos, Nigeria. It was pioneered by its three members Ben, Tola and Fola in the year 2020, and since then they have performed in all the 36 states of Nigeria. Over the years, WildCats has sold more than 100 record albums, and have been nominated twice for for AFRIMA awards winning in all two nominations. The band continues to grow and spread music in a magical way.
